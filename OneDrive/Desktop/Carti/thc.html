THC: THE HIGH CHRONICLE$ Details 
[Partially Lost] THE HIGH CHRONICLE$ (THC) by Playboi Carti / $ir Cartier

Back in 2011, a then 14-15 year old Playboi Carti (then known as $ir Cartier) released his debut mixtape THC (standing for The High Chronical$). Uploaded onto now-defunct hosting services and only receiving buzz from close friends and associates, it's not a shocker that this project has become lost. Only 2-3 out of the 8 songs are still available.

Important Details:
- Released: November 5, 2011
- Artist Name at the Time: $ir Cartier
- The project was announced by Carti on Twitter on October 14, 2011, with a planned release date of October 31, 2011 (later postponed).
- Originally hosted on limelinx (a now-dead song hosting site); the official link now redirects to a different site.
- A YouTube playlist (purportedly by Carti) once existed but was assembled from multiple channels.
- Discussions and leads about the mixtape have been found on:
    • Reddit: reddit.com/r/playboicarti/comments/ekhynp/possible_link_to_the_high_chronicles/
    • Leaked.cx: leaked.cx/threads/the-high-chronicles-playboi-carti-aka-sir-cartier.41328/
    • Genius: genius.com/albums/Playboi-carti/Thc-the-high-chronical
- A SoundCloud playlist with 3 songs exists (though widely circulated are only 2 tracks):
    m.soundcloud.com/user-20773527/sets/thc-the-high-chronicles-playboi-carti-ir-cartier-only-found-songs-circa-2011
- Many users recall that only tracks #2 and #5 are currently found, with the remaining tracks lost or privately held.
- Efforts to recover the mixtape via the Wayback Machine, Twitter searches, and discussions on Soulseek and Discord have so far been unsuccessful.
- There is proof the songs exist, but those who have them are unwilling to share them publicly without payment.
