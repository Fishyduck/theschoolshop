Background & Details

Young Mi$fit is the second mixtape by Atlanta rapper Playboi Carti, released under his early alias $ir Cartier. Announced on Twitter and released on November 12, 2012, the project was inspired by Carti’s experiences in the Atlanta scene. He stated that he originally made music for his friends and was heavily influenced by local culture.

Featuring: Nessly & Tom Voduz
