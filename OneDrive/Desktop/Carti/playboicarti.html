Background & Singles

Playboi Carti’s self‑titled mixtape marked his debut commercial project. Initially announced as "Cash Carti" by his then‑manager Ian Connor, the mixtape was released on April 14, 2017 via AWGE and Interscope Records. Two singles – "Lookin" (featuring Lil Uzi Vert) and "Wokeuplikethis" (featuring Lil Uzi Vert) – were released in early March 2017, while "Magnolia" and "Let It Go" were dropped on the same day as the mixtape to build anticipation.

The project features guest appearances from Lil Uzi Vert, ASAP Rocky, and Dutch singer Leven Kali. Production was primarily handled by Pi'erre Bourne, with additional contributions from Harry Fraud, Hit-Boy, Jake One, Ricci Riera, and Southside.

Critical Reception

The mixtape received generally favorable reviews, holding a Metacritic score of 69/100. Critics praised its innovative production, atmospheric soundscapes, and Carti’s confident, ad‑lib‑driven delivery. While some noted the project felt like a glorified beat tape, many appreciated its fresh and experimental approach that pushed the boundaries of trap music.

Commercial Performance

The mixtape debuted at number 12 on the US Billboard 200 with 28,000 album‑equivalent units (21,000 streaming units and 7,000 pure sales) in its first week. As of September 2017, it has moved over 367,000 units. It was certified Gold by the RIAA on January 10, 2018 and later certified Platinum on February 28, 2020.
