Background & Release

"Death in Tune" is a single release by Playboi Carti that dropped on May 13, 2015—almost a month after his hit single “Fetti.” The project features two tracks, "money counter" and "count it up," which are infused with Auto-Tune and boastful lyrics. Produced by Atlanta’s BeatPluggz and MexikoDro, the release is an early example of Carti’s signature style during his pre‑mainstream era.

Track Popularity

Although only a few tracks have survived from this release, "money counter" and "count it up" continue to circulate among fans and are frequently discussed on forums such as r/lostmedia. Their rarity has only added to the mystique of this early project.
